The Droplets

May 4, 2021 by Falguni

High above the sea

Where from roaring clouds

The cold breeze flee

The mist did shroud

Filled with glee

The newborn droplet proud

Notorious droplet to play

Jumped with friends

On a fine morning one day

Soon reaching the midway

Shined they with a smile, nascent

As the mist cleared

With the break of noon

The droplets cheered

As world ahead them shined like moon

But now they feared

As the sea approached soon

With the fall of dusk

The droplets touched sea

Becoming oblivious

They were now free

What remained the droplet or ocean?

Or just the silent eternal sea
